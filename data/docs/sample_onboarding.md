# Internal Support Playbook

## Password Reset

Support agents should verify the requester's identity before initiating a password reset. A reset link expires after 30 minutes and can only be used once.

## VPN Access

New employees receive VPN access after security training is completed. Managers must approve contractors before an access ticket is created.

## Escalation Policy

Critical production incidents should be escalated to the on-call engineering lead within 10 minutes. Include the customer impact, affected service, and first observed timestamp in the escalation note.
